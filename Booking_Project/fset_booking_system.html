About FSET

The Faculty of Science, Engineering & Technology at USTC

Est. 1992

University of Science and Technology Chittagong

USTC is located at the picturesque site near Foy's Lake, Chittagong. Since its inception, this institution has expanded a new sphere and scope in the industrial fields of medicine, pharmaceutical and biological sciences, engineering and technology, and business administration.

The Faculty of Science, Engineering & Technology (FSET) is home to three dynamic departments — CSE, EEE, and ETE — offering undergraduate programs designed to develop skilled engineers and researchers for the modern world.
