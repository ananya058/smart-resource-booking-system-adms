University of Science and Technology Chittagong (USTC)

USTC is located at the picturesque site near Foy's Lake. Since its inception, this institution has expanded a new sphere and a new scope in industrial fields of Medicine, pharmaceutical & biological sciences, engineering & technology and business administration in this soil of Chittagong, Bangladesh.
